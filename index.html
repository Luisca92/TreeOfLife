Welcome to my Website!


My name is Luis and I am a tropical biologist, naturalist, and wildlife photographer from Colombia researching tropical forest restoration.

My current research focuses on species selection for restoration plantings and how different assemblages influence the development of restoration plantings in landscapes fragmented by cattle pasture and agriculture. Presently I am headquartered in Medellín, Colombia and actively looking for postdoctoral opportunities in the fields of restoration and conservation.

Wildlife Sightings and Photography

As part of my work as a biologist, I am keen to contributing to wildlife sightings databases such as iNaturalist and Ebird. For those of you that do not yet partake in this practice, it is a phenomenal, adrenaline-filled way of learning the species around you. This basic of knowledge of "who is here" is also the very first step towards ecological research or action of any kind.
